Recipe of the Day:

Kabuli Pulao (کابلی پلو)

Kabuli Pulao is Afghanistan’s national dish. Fragrant rice cooked with lamb or beef, mixed with sweet carrots, raisins, and almonds. It’s a mix of sweet and savory flavors.

Popular Recipes

  • Kebab (grilled skewered meat)

    • Kabeb is a variety of grilled, skewered, or flat-pattied meats, most often lamb, but also beef and chicken, that are seasoned with onion, garlic, and spices like cumin and coriander.

  • Firni (milk custard)

    • Firni is a delicate and creamy rice pudding, is a beloved dessert in Afghan cuisine. Made with a few simple ingredients like milk, sugar, rice flour, and cardamom, this sweet treat offers a comforting and indulgent experience.

  • Afghan Green Tea

    • Afghan Green Tea is a traditional, aromatic green tea beverage brewed with green tea leaves and various spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and saffron.

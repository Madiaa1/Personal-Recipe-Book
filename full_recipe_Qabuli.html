Ingredients:

  • Rice - long-grain, like basmati
  • Meat - lamb, beef, or chicken
  • Carrots - sliced or julienned
  • Raisins - for sweetness
  • Almonds - sliced or whole
  • Onions - chopped
  • Garlic - minced (optional)
  • Oil or Ghee - for cooking
  • Salt and pepper - for taste
  • Spices - cardamom, cinnamon, cumin, or kabuli spice mix
  • Water or broth - to cook the rice and meat

Instructions:

  1. Cook the meat: Boil lamb or beef in water with a little salt until soft. Remove the meat and keep the broth.
  2. Cook the rice: Wash the rice. Boil it in the meat broth until soft, then drain.
  3. Prepare carrots and raisins: Fry sliced carrots in a little oil until soft. Add raisins and fry for 1‚Äì2 minutes.
  4. Mix meat and rice: Put cooked meat on the bottom of a large pot, then layer rice on top.
  5. Add toppings: Sprinkle fried carrots, raisins, and almonds on the rice.
  6. Steam together: Cover the pot and cook on very low heat for 10‚Äì15 minutes so flavors mix.
  7. Serve: Mix gently before serving. Enjoy!
